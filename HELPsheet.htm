Site Profiler is a GIS web app that gives you easy access to a lot of information about any property site you choose in Encinitas!

 

To use: 

Type an Address or APN in the FIND box…then click on the category tabs across the top of the map.

 

Below is a list of the information you can find in each tab:

 

ADDRESS INFO

·        Site Address(es) and SubAddress(es)*

·        Business Name(s)* & HDL Account Number(s)*

·        Site Type (e.g. residential, commercial, etc.)

 

 

ASSESSOR INFO

·        APN(s)

·        Owner Name

·        Mailing Address

·        Is Owner Occupied flag

·        Historic APN(s)*

 

 

 

ZONING

·        Base Zoning

·        General Plan Zoning

·        Specific Plan Zoning*

 

++ Button to launch detailed

      E-Zoning app ++

 

 

LAND RECORDS

·        Plan Name

·        Legal Date

·        County Recording (Year & Number)

·        Survey Date

·        Surveyor

·        Company

·        Plan Residential Development flag

·        Description

 

Subdivision Parcel Maps…
